BOX SHOOTER

A FPS game where you have to shoot green boxes to score within a time. There are also other boxes with different effect- yellow boxes decrease the time and white boxes increase the time. The game starts with a 30 seconds timer. The difficulty increases after every 5 seconds in which yellow boxes appear more frequently.

ROLLER MADNESS

A hamsterball-inspired game that you have to collect coins to reach the next level. There are also enemies that is spawning from time to time that is chasing you.
